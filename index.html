About

Friender


Frinder is an app that allows users to find other individuals on a map based on status or filters:

  • Letting users with accounts to select their current status when they are at a location.
  • Allow all app users to browse other users at different locations base on a filter.
  • Users then can choose to interact/chat with other users.
